University assignments are unlike anything from school. They require 
independent research, critical thinking, and strong time management skills.

## How I Manage My Assignments

- Always write every deadline in a planner the moment it is announced
- Break big assignments into smaller daily tasks
- Start research at least 5 days before the deadline
- Proofread every assignment at least twice before submitting
- Ask professors for help early — never wait until the night before

## Dealing With Exam Stress

Exam season is the most intense time of a student's year. The key is to treat 
stress as energy and channel it productively.

### My Stress Relief Methods

**Take a walk** — 10 minutes of fresh air resets your mind completely.

**Talk to someone** — Never bottle up stress alone. A conversation with a 
friend or family member makes everything feel lighter.

**Gratitude journal** — Every night I write three positive things from my day, 
even during exam week. It shifts your mindset from panic to gratitude.

## The Most Important Advice

Pressure is not the enemy. Procrastination is. Start early, stay calm, and 
always finish strong.
