The campus is more than a place to attend classes. It is a world unto itself 
— a community filled with energy, ideas, and endless possibility.

## My Favourite Spots on Campus

### The Library
My sanctuary for deep focus and long study sessions. I love the peaceful 
atmosphere and the vast collection of books available to explore.

### The Cafeteria
Where friendships are built over chai and snacks. The social hub of campus 
life — always full of laughter and conversation.

### Campus Gardens
My favourite spot to sit and think between classes. The greenery is calming 
and beautiful, perfect for clearing your mind.

### Sports Ground
Where I clear my head with a game of cricket. Physical activity is absolutely 
essential for maintaining mental wellbeing during stressful times.

## What Campus Life Taught Me

Campus life showed me that education happens everywhere — not just in 
classrooms. It happens in gardens, cafeterias, corridors, and in every 
conversation with someone different from yourself.

Someday I will graduate and leave this campus behind. But I know I will miss 
every corner of it for the rest of my life.
